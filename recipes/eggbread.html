Eggbread Recipe

Eggbread, healthy and yummy!

Looking for a juicy dish at work? Here you go with nice, good food for heart and soul. Try it out!

Ingredients:

  • 1 bread
  • 4 pcs m-sized eggs
  • salad sauce
  • salt
  • fresh cress

Steps:

  1. cut bread into slices
  2. boil eggs hard meanwhile
  3. peel eggs and cut into slices
  4. spread out eggslices on the bread and salt them
  5. cover eggs with salad sauce distribute some cress on it
  6. Bon Appetite!
