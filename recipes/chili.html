Chili Recipe

Chili for connoisseur!

Hot Chili for real men. Beware and try out at your own risk!

Ingredients:

  • 1kg grounded beef
  • 1 can sieved tomatos
  • 100ml red wine
  • 100ml vegetable broth
  • 150g corn
  • 250g beans
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 10 carolina reaper

Steps:

  1. heat some oil in a pot and roast the meat
  2. extinguish with red wine and let it boil
  3. add sieved tomatos and let it simmer
  4. add remaining ingredients and let it simmer at least for 3 hours
  5. Serve with white bread and milk
  6. Bon Appetite!
